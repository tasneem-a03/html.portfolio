The Best Books According to Tasnim

My top 3 books of all-time

The Fine print

This is my favourite book. This book is by Lauren Asher. And it is about billionare whose name is Rowan and he is grumpy and a girl name Zahra who is sweet and gives sunshine kinda vibe

Twisted hate

This is also my favourite book. This book is by Ana Huang. And this is the story about Alex and Ava. Again Billionare, Grumpy-sunshine trope.

The Love Hypothesis

This is also my favourite book by Ali Hazelwood. I think we all have been in a phase where we've fallen in love with some teacher... This book brings it to reality! A really fun, light-hearted, sensual and romantic story. Plus, Ali Hazelwood has this very fun, light way of writing that makes the whole book even more fun. It was a pure delight reading it, and I highly recommend it to anyone who loves rom-coms and who are hopelessly romantic, just like me!
